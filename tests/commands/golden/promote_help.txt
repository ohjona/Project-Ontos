usage: ontos_promote.py [-h] [--check] [--all-ready] [--quiet] [files ...]

Promote documents from Level 0/1 to Level 2.

positional arguments:
  files        Specific files to promote

optional arguments:
  -h, --help   show this help message and exit
  --check      Show promotable documents without changing anything
  --all-ready  Batch promote all documents that are ready
  --quiet, -q  Minimal output
